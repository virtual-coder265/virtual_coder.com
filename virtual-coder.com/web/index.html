Register for our exclusive membership by filling out our sign-up form with your valid information. Your privacy is of utmost importance to us, and any data you provide will never be shared with any other party, unless otherwise agreed upon by you.

Step 2: Payment Options

Choose a Secure Payment Method

After successful registration, you will be asked to deposit an amount equivalent to your plan. Payment methods may vary depending on your location. For local scholars, we accept Airtel Money, TNM Mpamba, Standard Bank, and National Bank. For international scholars, payments are made through Paypal and GPay.

To ensure the safety and security of your payment, please adhere strictly to the following measures:

  • All payments should be triggered and authorized by you through your third-party service of choice.
  • We do not process any auto-renewals, so to renew your subscription plan, you will have to initiate and process the payment once ready.
  • DO NOT share any of your payment information, such as credit card number, SSN, account details, or PIN, with us, unless specifically stated otherwise.
